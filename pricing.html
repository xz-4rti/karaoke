Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. For yearly subscriptions, we also offer payment via bank transfer.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for both our Premium and Pro plans. No credit card is required to start your trial, and you can downgrade to the Basic plan before the trial ends if you don't wish to continue.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. After cancellation, you'll still have access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer discounts for teams or groups?
Yes, we offer special pricing for groups of 10 or more users. Please contact our sales team through the Contact page for more information about our group rates.
