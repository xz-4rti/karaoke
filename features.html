Our Features

Discover what makes our virtual karaoke platform the perfect choice for music lovers around the world. We've designed every feature with your enjoyment in mind, creating the ultimate online karaoke experience.

Real-time Voice Chat

Our advanced audio processing technology ensures crystal-clear sound with minimal latency, making it feel like you're in the same room with your friends.

  • High-quality audio streaming up to 320kbps
  • Automatic noise cancellation for background sounds
  • Echo reduction for professional sound quality
  • Voice enhancement features to make you sound your best
  • Adjustable microphone sensitivity and volume controls

YouTube Integration

Access millions of karaoke tracks directly from YouTube without ever leaving our platform. Our synchronized playback ensures everyone experiences the music together.

  • Search and play any karaoke track from YouTube
  • Create and save playlists for your favorite songs
  • Synchronized playback for all room participants
  • Lyrics display for sing-along experience
  • Queue system for organizing who sings next

Private Rooms

Create your own private karaoke space where you control who joins and how the session runs. Perfect for virtual parties with friends and family.

  • Password-protected rooms for privacy
  • Customizable room settings and appearance
  • Host controls for managing participants
  • Capacity for up to 50 participants per room
  • Room persistence - save your room for future sessions

Live Chat

Stay connected with other participants through our real-time chat system. Share reactions, request songs, and enjoy the social aspect of karaoke.

  • Real-time messaging with all room participants
  • Emoji reactions and animated GIFs
  • Song request system integrated with chat
  • Private messaging between participants
  • Chat moderation tools for room hosts
